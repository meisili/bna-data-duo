About the Project

The story behind our investigation into the chemicals hidden in everyday beauty products.

Inspiration

This project began with a simple question: What are we actually putting on our skin? Our project hopes to create transparency into the cosmetic products consumers use every day and the hazardous traits associated with their use.

Methodology

The Data Analysis: We analyzed data on reported products & reportable ingredients from the California Department of Public Health. We identified products' associated harms by scraping data from Common Chemistry's API.

Technical Stack: This website was built using:

  • Structure: HTML & CSS
  • Interactivity: JavaScript & VegaLite
  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)

Sources & Data

Our story data was sourced from:

  • Product & Ingredient Data: California Safe Cosmetics Program (CSCP) Product Database
  • Hazard Trait Data: Reportable Ingredients Exception List
  • Chemical Data: Common Chemistry Database & API
